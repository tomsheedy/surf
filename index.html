PACKAGES

To us, surfing is not only about riding a wave, but exploring new places, adventure, meeting new people and making friends around the world - we want you to become a part of it!

We offer a variety of packages that suit absolutely anyone, even if you haven't surfed before!

Lessons are tailored to individuals to provide the most effective tutelage, and we provide photo feedback sessions to help you identify any weaknesses you might have in order to improve.

We also provide surf guiding packages to show the experienced the very best surf spots in the continent, tailored to each days forcast of course.

All packages come with optional yoga lessons, which are especially tailored to surfers, and have proved very beneficial to increasing your surfing potential, as well as your overall wellbeing.
