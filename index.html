About Us
Offshore surf specialises in delivering tailor made surf lessons for all abilities, and surf guiding for advanced surfers; consistently providing unrivaled, unforgettable and stress free surf holidays is what we do best.

Our surf store is well located in Tamraght, a small surf and fishing village situated in between Taghazout and Aourir (banana village), and just 15km north of Agadir.

We offer a variety of services at the surf centre from Surf lessons, board and wetsuit hire, to in-store surf theory, accommodation, care hire, quad excursions, horse and camel trekking, desert trips, Atlas Mountain hiking, city tours and much more.

We pride ourselves on our quality, personal services. We are following our passion, and we want to share that passion with you!
