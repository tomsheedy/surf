Beginners Package
If you’re new to surfing, or still mastering the white water, let our highly qualified and experienced instructors introduce you to the world of wave riding! In two hours you will be amazed at how much you can progress, and most of our students are standing in the first lesson. Our instructors will be at your side, helping you the whole way with advice and encouragement. We understand that everybody learns differently and our instructors have been trained to provide top quality, individual surf lessons whatever your ability and learning style. Go at your own pace, lessons can be challenging but are always amazing fun!!

You’ll have the photo evidence to show off your new skills… however we take photos not only to show how amazing you are, but to enable you to improve by watching yourself in the waves. We will hold a photo feedback session where our instructors will take a look at your style, technique and any weaknesses, so that you can work on these to progress with your surfing level. You’ll be amazed at how much this helps with learning to surf!

Our surf instructors are qualified both with the International Surf Association (ISA) and the Surf Life Saving Association (SLSA). For every one of them, surfing is their passion and life spirit! You’ll see this radiating from them when they teach you and it’s definitely infectious! All you need to do is bring your towel, waterproof sunblock, bounds of energy and a good sense of humour. Leave the rest to us!
