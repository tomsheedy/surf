Intermediate Surf Lessons

Mbarek is trained with the International Surf Association (ISA) Level 2 and the Royal Life Saving Society (RLSS) and with over 10 years of teaching experience, you couldn't be in better hands. He will help you to advance your surfing level with a variety of different breaks to work with.

We will find the top conditions each day to suit your level, enabling you to progress and make the advances in your surfing that we are striving for, whilst always keeping you safe. The intermediate course is designed for goal orientated surf lessons, with the use of photo/video analysis and feedback, looking at the essential skills necessary to become a great surfer.

Intermediate surf lesson overview:

  • Analysis of current surfing ability
  • Technical surf theory and ocean awareness
  • Safe entry and exits from the water and understanding each spot
  • Improving padding technique, sitting and general board control, including duck diving
  • Perfecting different bottom turns to adapt to the structure of the waves
  • Fine tuning your timing and wave selection
  • How to improve your line up positioning and surf etiquette reminder
  • Body positioning for maneuvers, working on the cut back, off the lip and floater
  • How to gain speed, trimming and creating fluid carving turns
  • Pinpointing any weaknesses and creating remedy exercises to overcome them
  • Video/photo feedback and de-briefing

Surf Guiding

Providing an unrivalled surf guiding experience on the North African Atlantic coast, you will be driven to the best spots for your ability, from Tiffnit to Imsouane. Working around the tides, we get to each spot when the conditions are at their peak, making sure you get the best of the surf each day.

Your safety is always our priority, and our local guides will surf with you, keeping you well informed about the specifics of each surf spot. They are also there to offer advice and help with anything from equipment needs, to specific aspects of your surfing which may need tweaking. Show them any maneuvers you are having difficulty with and our guides will be able to pinpoint exactly what needs fixing to get it spot on.

Offshore Surf Science

There is so much more to surfing than riding a wave, and if we can show you a little of the beauty and power of the ocean, and how to better understand it and to stay safe, then we have done our job well.

Mbarek will talk you through everything from currents and learning how to negotiate them, to wave formation, tides and how the moon plays its part, wind and ground swell, the different types of breaks (beach, reef, point..) how to read a surf report, surf technique for different abilities, the range of different surf boards, how to choose what’s best for your ability and ambitions in the water, and much, much more.

With the use of diagrams and boards in-store for surf technique practice, you will leave feeling ready to hit those waves in confidence, whatever the conditions. These sessions are always brilliant fun too!

Accommodation in Tamraght

Choose between staying at the traditional Moroccan Riad or the Surfer's Apartments opposite the Offshore surf shop.

The Riad

Nestled at the very bottom of the Anti Atlas Mountains is our main accommodation in Tamraght, a traditional Berber and tranquil surf village with amazing charm situated next to Taghazout, just 15km north of Agadir. The perfect place to chill after a day in the water, with ocean and mountain views, unbeatable Moroccan meals and great company.

  • Choose between our en-suite double, twin or 3/4 bed family rooms.
  • Delicious fresh and varied Moroccan breakfasts and traditional locally sourced home cooked dinners
  • Open and spacious rooftop terrace with sun beds and cosy sofas
  • Swimming pool and poolside dining area
  • Beautiful ocean view yoga terrace
  • Free Wifi

Surfer's Apartments

Located in Tamraght opposite Offshore surf shop, and just a 10 minute walk to our local beach break, the surfers apartments offer your own peaceful hideaway in the hub of the village.

Our apartments each have 2 bedrooms with a double bed, so accommodate 4 people per apartment. There is a kitchen, dining area, living room and bathroom. Enjoy the sunset on the beautiful rooftop terrace with views over the ocean.

About Us

We are proud to introduce you to Offshore Surf Morocco! Meet the team behind it.

Mbarek Co-founder and head surf coach

With 10 years of surf coaching experience, ISA and RLSS qualifications, Mbarek puts everyone at ease and delivers spot on adjustments, advice and feedback both in and out of the water.

A local to the area, he knows where and when to surf for any ability and all conditions – famous, secret, reef, point, beach… you name it, he will find you it.

Known as the kamikaze here, for fearlessly surfing BIG waves!

Charlotte Co-founder, Yoga teacher and host.

A resident since 2015, she is your go to person when you are here, will make you feel great after yoga and at home right away.

She has surfed the Moroccan beginner and intermediate spots enough to know the score, and will certainly give you a whole lot of encouragement out in the lineup! Trained with 200hrs in Anusara yoga, Charlotte's yoga classes are delivered from the heart, adapting to each and everybody's energy levels to create comfort and balance after a day's surfing.
