The home of surf progression in Morocco

Committed to bringing you the ultimate surf adventure in Morocco, we provide surf holidays for all levels, surf guiding and a cultural experience not to be rivaled. We are not a big surf centre; rather we pride ourselves on the quality, personal service we consistently provide.

Packages

Our region boasts an impressive 160km of North Atlantic coastline bursting with a spectacular array of surf spots, to suit absolutely any ability. We will create a unique surf package to suit you, and to give you the best possible experience, whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced surfer waiting to hit the famous breaks Morocco has to offer!

Intermediate Surf Lessons

Our surf instructors will help you to build on your surfing experience with a variety of different breaks to work with. We will find you the optimal wave conditions each day to suit your level, to enable you to progress and make the advances in your surfing that we are striving for, whilst always keeping you safe.

The intermediate course is designed for goal orientated surf lessons, with the use of photo/video analysis and feedback, looking at the essential skills necessary to become a great surfer. The benefits of seeing yourself in replay are endless and your surfing level will certainly improve in a minimal amount of time, with slow motion replay footage!

Intermediate surf lesson overview:

  • Analysis of current surfing ability
  • Improving padding technique, sitting and general board control
  • Perfecting your bottom turn to adapt to faster breaking waves
  • Fine tuning your timing and wave selection
  • How to improve your line up positioning and surf etiquette
  • Body positioning for maneuvers, working on the cut back, off the lip and floater
  • How to gain speed, trimming and creating fluid carving turns
  • Video/photo feedback and de-briefing
  • Technical surf theory/Safe entry and exits from the water

Surf Guiding

Providing an unrivalled surf guiding experience on the North African Atlantic coast, you will be driven to the best spots for your ability, from Tiffnit to Imsouane. Working around the tides, we get to each spot when the conditions are at their peak, making sure you get the best of the surf each day.

Your safety is always our priority, and our local guides will surf with you, keeping you well informed about the specifics of each surf spot. They are also there to offer advice and help with anything from equipment needs, to specific aspects of your surfing which may need tweaking. Show them any maneuvers you are having difficulty with and our guides will be able to pinpoint exactly what needs fixing to get it spot on.

Offshore Surf Centre Surf Theory

There is so much more to surfing than riding a wave, and if we can show you a little of the beauty and power of the ocean, and how to better understand it and to stay safe, then we have done our job well.

Mbarek will talk you through everything from currents and learning how to negotiate them, to wave formation, tides and how the moon plays its part, wind and ground swell, the different types of breaks (beach, reef, point..) how to read a surf report, surf technique for different abilities, the range of different surf boards, how to choose what’s best for your ability and ambitions in the water, and much, much more.

With the use of diagrams and boards in-store for surf technique practice, you will leave feeling ready to hit those waves in confidence, whatever the conditions. These sessions are always brilliant fun too!
