The home of surf progression in Morocco

Committed to bringing you the ultimate surf adventure in Morocco, we provide surf holidays for all levels, surf guiding and a cultural experience not to be rivaled. We are not a big surf centre; rather we pride ourselves on the quality, personal service we consistently provide.

Packages

Our region boasts an impressive 160km of North Atlantic coastline bursting with a spectacular array of surf spots, to suit absolutely any ability. We will create a unique surf package to suit you, and to give you the best possible experience, whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced surfer waiting to hit the famous breaks Morocco has to offer!

If you’re new to surfing or still mastering the white water, let our surf instructors introduce you to the waves and transform you into a surfer. We understand that everybody learns differently, so our instructors have been trained to provide top quality, tailor made surf lessons whatever your ability and learning style - they will always be in the water with you for support and encouragement.

Go at your own pace, lessons can be challenging but are always amazing fun!! Look out for our photographer on the beach, it’s always priceless to look at photos after dinner, and so beneficial to your progress in the water!

What’s included:

  • Return Agadir transfers (min 5 night stay)
  • Surf transport to the best spots according to your ability and the conditions each day
  • 2.5 hour surf lesson
  • Supervised free surf sessions after lunch
  • All surf equipment
  • Accommodation at Villa Azur
  • Traditional Moroccan breakfasts and dinners
  • Lunch and water at the beach
  • Free wifi at the Villa
  • Offshore surf guide on hand 24/7
